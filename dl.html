What's next ?

My current research interests and work is in deep learning and its associated technology stack. Within deep learning, I remain focused on CV applications. I have re-visited numerous problems that were not solved to my satisfaction and with the power of the deep networks, I have started looking at problems in a different way. Most of my current work is protected by NDA and propreitary technology which cannot be shared outside of work.

Deep learning is a vast field and there is plenty of avenues to explore. I chose to explore the field of unsupervised, semi-supervised, few shot learning . On top of thes, AI assisted labeling, augmentation seem to excite me more than just training models to solve CV applications.

At cognex we help our customers develop deep learning based industrial applications in a very easier way with little friction as sometimes it might be daunting for folks without ML experience. This way our customers can focus on the core problem and worry less about the ML part of the problem
