About me

Thanks for visiting this page. I'm currently working as a Principal Enginner at Cognex Corporation. I work on deep learning software and solutions applied to the machine vision industry

I'm a passionate CV Engineer and have 10+ years of experience in solving practical problems using computer vision and machine learning

I graduated in June 2011 from University of Illinois at Chicago with my masters in Electrical and Computer Engineering

My masters thesis was part of the NSF funded project which aimed at assisting elderly patients via multi model communication with robots using vision, speech and haptics

My research work "An Effecient Methodology for 3D Tracking & Pointing Localiation" was advised by Jezekiel Ben-Arie
