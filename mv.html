Machine Vision Applications

Over the years I have developed and worked on numerous vision applications that span various industries such as food, packaging, biomedical, pharma, logistics, consumer electronics, automotive etc. Most of these applicatons are never to be seen outside the walls of manufacturing

I have embedded videos of some of these applications when they were being built or during validation stages

Kinect guided robotic bin picking

This system used 3D depth information from the Kinect V1 sensor to guide an industrial robot to do bin picking. The kinect gets triggered continuously and updates the robot on the next best pick position based on a 4x4 grid




Inspection of Tire wheel assemblies

This system has two main components. One is to check the integrity of the tire seated on wheel. This system uses a laser profiler based on acquisiton to recreate a zmap of the tire sidewall which is inspected for defects. The second component is the verification of the wheel pattern which is performed on a 2D image.



High Precision inspection of Orings

This system uses a 4ft telecentric lens to gauge orings to 1/100th of a mm.
