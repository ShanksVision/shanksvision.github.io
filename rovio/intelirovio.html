Obstacle avoidance by Rovio

Rovio comes with the IR Radar but it never utilizes it. Here I create a simple algorithm for rovio to navigate autonomously in the room without colliding the obstacles. I get the value of the IR Radar sensor continously from the rovio. Once i get the obstalce i turn rovio 20 degrees to left till there is no obstacle. Take a look how rovio avoids the obstacles..
